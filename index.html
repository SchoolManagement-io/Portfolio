Pick & Drop Van

Tech Stack: C#, .Net, MySQL

A service-based web application designed to facilitate quick and reliable vehicle repair support through three user roles: Vehicle Owner, Mechanic, and Garage Owner. Vehicle owners can submit issues related to their vehicle and choose between two options—requesting a mechanic for on-site repair or scheduling a vehicle tow. Based on the selection, real-time notifications along with the owner's location are sent to nearby mechanics or garage owners. Once the request is accepted, both parties can proceed with the service. The system focuses on fast communication, service flexibility, and location-based request handling to improve roadside assistance and repair experiences.
